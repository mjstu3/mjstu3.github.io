about us

We are the Mars brothers, Matjaž and Lygo! We grew up together in the Netherlands and discovered a passion for heart-thumping music. We moved to the city of Melbourne in 2017 to pursue our dreams of dropping new beats and making sick remixes for everyone. Electronic music is more than a genre, it's a lifestyle.

There are many ways which you can support us. Please purchase our music and attend our performances on tours! You can also follow us on social media to keep up to date with all Mars news and tour information.

Follow us on our mission to share our love of electronic dance music to the people of Victoria!
